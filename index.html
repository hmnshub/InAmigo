Who We Are

Founded in 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization committed to creating lasting social impact. With its base in Chhattisgarh, the foundation operates across India, addressing critical societal issues through a network of dedicated professionals and volunteers.

We believe in the power of collective action. Our transparent operations and strong partnerships enable us to bring real, measurable change to society.
