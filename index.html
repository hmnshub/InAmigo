Who We Are

Founded in 2020, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization based in Chhattisgarh, operating across India. We believe in the power of collective action to address critical societal issues.

Through our dedicated volunteer network, we focus on essential causes such as child education, women's empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare, and food distribution. We are proud to be ISO 9001:2015 certified and recognized by NITI Aayog.

Our Ongoing Projects & Impact

Project Prakriti

Impact: Environmental conservation and sustainability.

Emphasizing that each tree is a gift to future generations, we advocate for eco-friendly agriculture. Under this project, we have successfully planted over 20,000+ saplings to provide cleaner air and a healthier planet.

Project Bachpanshala

Impact: Nurturing young minds through quality education.

We are bridging educational gaps by training underprivileged children in basic digital literacy, life skills, and school education support to help break the cycle of poverty.

Project Udaan

Impact: Women empowerment and financial independence.

Collaborating with self-help groups in rural areas, we promote skill development and menstrual hygiene awareness. We have empowered over 900+ girls through our targeted campaigns.

Project Seva

Impact: Serving humanity with compassion.

Dedicated to providing essential resources to those in need, we have distributed over 50,000+ meals and clothing to underprivileged individuals across the community.
