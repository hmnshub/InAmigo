Project Vikas

Cultivating the Leaders of Tomorrow.

Written by Himanshu Kumar Singh

F

or the past four years, InAmigos Foundation has stood at the critical intersection of education and employability. The journey from a university classroom to a thriving career is rarely a straight line. Every year, millions of bright, ambitious minds graduate into a world that demands experience they haven’t yet been allowed to earn.

The word Vikas translates to 'progress.' We launched this initiative to completely dismantle the barrier between raw academic potential and real-world success. We don't just believe in giving students a chance; we believe in giving them a launchpad. And the response has been nothing short of a quiet revolution. We have engaged with over 100,000 eager minds, successfully onboarding more than 30,000 interns into meaningful, transformative roles.

A Canvas of Opportunities

We recognize that true progress does not come from a one-size-fits-all approach. Every student harbors a unique talent waiting to be refined. For the creative soul, we provide the space to master storytelling through Content Writing, Graphic Design, and Video Editing. For the strategic thinker, we offer deep-dives into Digital Marketing, Market Research, and Public Relations.

We immerse our youth in the deep end of real-world challenges. Whether they are learning the art of talent acquisition in HR, managing live events, or shaping future startups through entrepreneurship programs, they are not just completing tasks—they are building their professional identity.

"True progress happens when we stop asking the youth what they lack, and start providing the platforms they deserve."

Beyond the Desk

But a true career requires holistic nurturing. An internship is only the beginning. At Project Vikas, we actively go beyond daily responsibilities. We surround our interns with industry experts through live seminars and webinars. We provide platforms like talent shows to celebrate their unique abilities, and we sit down with them for rigorous resume building and interview preparation.

We aren't just handing out certificates; we are equipping them with the unshakeable confidence needed to walk into any interview room and command respect.

A Pan-India Movement

Today, the heartbeat of Project Vikas can be felt across every corner of India. There is no greater joy for our team than watching our alumni transition from our virtual desks into full-time, impactful careers at leading organizations. They leave us not just as former interns, but as capable, empowered leaders ready to shape the future workforce.
